PAC112VM

Rp3.710.000

*Retail price (including VAT)

The Yamaha PAC112VM Pacifica electric guitar is a great beginner instrument for younger, emerging players at an incredible price. The PAC112VM features a lightweight alder body with a smooth maple neck and a snappy maple fingerboard. A killer trio of versatile pickups lets you dial in a variety of tones from clean highs to punchy midrange to low-end growl. Loaded for sound.

  • Alder body with a gloss polyurethane finish
  • Bolt-on maple neck with maple fingerboard
  • The Alnico V pickup delivers warm, clear tones
  • H/S/S pickup configuration with 5-position selector
  • Push-pull coil-split switch for additional tone options
  • Vintage style vibrato bridge with block saddle
